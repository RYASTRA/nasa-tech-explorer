Autonomous navigation is a critical piece of technology in advancing NASA’s lunar missions. Autonomous navigation is advantageous in reducing network communication load while providing real-time positioning and telemetry. The NavCube 2.0 dual-frequency GPS L1/L2C receiver combines an addition frequency receiver along with proven heritage technology that forms the future of space GPS systems.
