Antenna Arraying Capable of Handling Very High Data Rates In Real Time

Case number
GSC-TOPS-296
NASA center
GSFC
Category
electrical and electronics
First seen
2026-07-20

NASA is increasingly designing payloads that require telecommunication transmissions. These payloads increase the competition for telecommunication bandwidth between space and earth. However, increasing the transmission data rate leads to a higher noise to signal ratios. The innovation is an antenna design that enables large amounts of data to be sent and received at a high data rate.
