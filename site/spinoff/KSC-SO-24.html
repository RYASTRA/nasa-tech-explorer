Another industrial spinoff O.Z. Gedney Co., Terryville, CT found the answer to a problem in a NASA Tech Brief describing research in adhesive bonding for the Space Shuttle. Gedney which makes electrical fittings for industrial plants was developing a fire stop a device that prevents the spread of fire through holes where cables and pipes penetrate fire barriers in buildings. At the company's request NASA supplied a technical information package which identified the best adhesive and the proper bonding technique.
