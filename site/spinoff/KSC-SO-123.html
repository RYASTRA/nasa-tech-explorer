Modified Fittings Enhance Industrial Safety

Case number
KSC-SO-123
NASA center
KSC
Category
public safety
First seen
2026-07-20

Concerned with the safety of the connections on pressurized air hoses, engineers at Kennedy Space Center modified a fitting to control the air supply hose in the event of an accidental release. An Enid, Oklahoma-based business, PT Coupling Company, licensed the technology and incorporated it into a new line of products to automatically prevent rapid separation of the fittings, enhancing worker safety.
