Two engineers at Glenn Research Center developed a tiny, implantable transmitter and handheld reader. Endotronix of Naperville, Illinois, now part of Edwards Lifesciences, licensed the patent and used it to develop a heart monitor that warns heart failure patients of an impending crisis.
