Mass Analyzers Facilitate Research on Addiction

Case number
KSC-SO-122
NASA center
KSC
Category
health and medicine
First seen
2026-07-20

To enhance the technology that monitored hydrogen and oxygen levels in the orbiter while on the launch pad, Kennedy Space Center awarded an SBIR contract to Houston-based Ionwerks Inc. After the SBIR, Ionwerks incorporated many of the innovations into its commercial laser imaging spectrometer. Today, Ionwerks sells the spectrometer for biological mass spectrometry and basic biological research.
