Thermal Insulation Strips Conserve Energy

Case number
KSC-SO-109
NASA center
KSC
Category
environment and resource management
First seen
2026-07-20

Flexible aerogel technology developed by Aspen Aerogels, of Northborough, Massachusetts, under SBIR contracts with Kennedy Space Center is now being employed in a building insulation application by Tampa, Florida-based Acoustiblok Inc. The company's Thermablok flexible aerogel strips-easy-to-install and environmentally friendly-are applied to wall studs in buildings to break the thermal bridging between the interior and exterior walls, boosting the insulation factor by as much as 42 percent.
