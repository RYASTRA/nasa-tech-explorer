Polymers Advance Heat Management Materials for Vehicles

Case number
MSFC-SO-206
NASA center
MSFC
Category
transportation
First seen
2026-07-20

Under NASA funding, Alliant Techsystems Inc. contracted with Starfire Systems Inc. in Schenectady, New York, to supply a polymer material to incorporate into a formula to repair damage on the exterior of the space shuttle. Called SMP-10, the polymer converts into a ceramic at high temperatures. Starfire now manufactures NASA-derived heat management products for military, aerospace, aviation, and automotive markets.
