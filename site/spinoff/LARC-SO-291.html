New Solar Array Design Saves Space

NASA center

First seen

NASA plans to use solar electric propulsion to send astronauts to Mars, but the technology will require huge solar arrays that take up precious space in a rocket fairing. So a NASA team invented the Compact Telescoping Array concept, which can reduce a stowed solar array’s volume by a third. Now a Northrop Grumman facility in Goleta, California, is producing arrays based on NASA’s design.
