Shuttle Repair Tools Automate Vehicle Maintenance

Case number
KSC-SO-128
NASA center
KSC
Category
transportation
First seen
2026-07-20

When paperwork generated by shuttle repairs got out of hand, Kennedy Space Center developed software that would streamline the maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) process. The result was SMART, a tool that automates MRO, cutting repair times by 45 percent. Orlando, Florida-based Diversified Industries Inc. licensed SMART and now offers it commercially.
