Time-Triggered Ethernet Slims Down Critical Data Systems

Case number
JSC-SO-299
NASA center
JSC
Category
transportation
First seen
2026-07-20

Ethernet traditionally could not be used for critical systems because it couldn’t guarantee when or whether signals would reach their destinations. TTTech, whose U.S. headquarters is in Andover, Massachusetts, developed a more reliable system, called Time-Triggered Ethernet, which it honed under a Johnson Space Center subcontract to build avionics for the Orion capsule. The technology, which allows for far simpler data handling on fewer computers, is now applied to launch vehicles, planes, wind turbines, and driver assistance and self-driving cars.
