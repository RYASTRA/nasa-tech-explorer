Electrical Body Signals Help Researchers Restore Movement and More

Case number
JSC-SO-359
NASA center
JSC
Category
health and medicine
First seen
2026-07-20

NASA has long studied the effects of weightlessness on astronauts’ muscle functioning. With support from Johnson Space Center, including SBIR contracts, Delsys Inc. of Natick, Massachusetts, has developed smart, wireless technology for sensing electromyographic signals to map motor nerve activity in the body. Customers are working with the devices to improve control of prosthetics, reverse paralysis, restore speech abilities, and design athletic gear.
