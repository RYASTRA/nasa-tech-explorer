To develop radiation sensors for deep space missions, Johnson Space Center provided Small Business Innovation Research funding to Tech-X Corporation in Boulder, Colorado, to design software that reliably simulates how radiation travels, which is useful for commercial space, industrial manufacturing, and medical devices.
