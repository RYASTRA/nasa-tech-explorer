Alloy-Enhanced Fans Maintain Fresh Air in Tunnels

Case number
MSFC-SO-198
NASA center
MSFC
Category
public safety
First seen
2026-07-20

Marshall Space Flight Center developed a high-strength aluminum alloy three to four times stronger than conventional aluminum alloys at high temperatures. Twin City Fan Companies Ltd. in Minneapolis, Minnesota, licensed the alloy and is currently employing the technology to make impellers for safety ventilation fans in rail and road tunnels.
