Space Radiation Research Fights Cancer on Earth

Case number
JSC-SO-356
NASA center
JSC
Category
health and medicine
First seen
2026-07-20

Protecting astronauts from space radiation exposure is important, so Johnson Space Center funded research into using certain segments of DNA to estimate radiation damage. After it proved successful, Promega Corp. in Fitchburg, Wisconsin, used the technique to create its OncoMate MSI Dx Analysis System diagnostic test which is used to customize cancer treatment.
