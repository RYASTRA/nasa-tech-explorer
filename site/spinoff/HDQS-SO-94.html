Heated Goggles

Case number
HDQS-SO-94
NASA center
HDQS
Category
consumer/home/recreation
First seen
2026-07-20

Sierracin Corporation has incorporated NASA technology onto their electrically heated ski-goggles that provide fog-free sight where activity demands total vision. Defogging is accomplished by applying heat to prevent moisture condensation; electric heat is supplied by a small battery built into goggles' headband. Heat is spread across lenses by means of invisible coating of electrically conductive metallic film.
