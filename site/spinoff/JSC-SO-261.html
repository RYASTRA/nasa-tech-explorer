Ultra-Thin Coatings Beautify Art

Case number
JSC-SO-261
NASA center
JSC
Category
consumer/home/recreation
First seen
2026-07-20

Early in the space program, Johnson Space Center developed a vacuum-deposition method for applying ultra-thin coatings of metal to substances. The technology spun off into commercial applications, such as the manufacture of dichroic glass. Jan Lewczenko, owner of Mount Pleasant, Pennsylvania-based JL Crystal Artistry LLC makes use of dichroic glass in his crystal sculptures.
