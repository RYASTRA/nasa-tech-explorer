Cadillac Seville Using NASTRAN

Cadillac Seville was first General Motors car designed by company's Vehicle Structural Analysis Program based in part on NASTRAN. Computer program improved car's ride within weight limits and saved development time. Success of Seville inspired company to extend computer analysis to the entire GM line.
