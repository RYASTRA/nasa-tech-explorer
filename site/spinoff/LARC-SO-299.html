In the 1960s, Langley Research Center oversaw a comprehensive investigation of the shape memory alloy nitinol, research that was foundational to nitonol products from Metalwerks, including a new formulation. The Aliquippa, Pennsylvania-based company supplies the medical field and high-tech manufacturing industries with the shape memory alloy.
