Speech Recognition Interfaces Improve Flight Safety

Case number

NASA center
LARC
Category
transportation
First seen

Entering a flight plan into a GPS is time-consuming and poses a safety hazard for pilots. VoiceFlight Systems LLC, in Troy, New York, created a speech recognition interface to replace manual inputs. With support from an SBIR contract from Langley Research Center, the VSF101 became the first Federal Aviation Administration-certified speech recognition product for use in civilian aircraft.
