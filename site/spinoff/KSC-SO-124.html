Software Programs Derive Measurements from Photographs

Case number
KSC-SO-124
NASA center
KSC
Category
consumer/home/recreation
First seen
2026-07-20

A scientist at Kennedy Space Center devised a software program to calculate the unknown dimension of objects in photographs to assist in the investigation following the Columbia disaster. In 2008, DigiContractor Corporation of Tarzana, California, licensed the technology for a product line that can calculate measurements from photos for applications from landscaping and flooring projects to crime scenes and auto accidents.
