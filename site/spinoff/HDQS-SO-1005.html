To create programming teaching materials, Microsoft Corp. of Redmond, Washington, collaborated with the Office of STEM Engagement at NASA Headquarters under a Space Act Agreement. By using NASA data and imagery to help create educational modules for their Microsoft Learn webpage, hundreds of thousands of students have learned to program with inspiration from NASA missions.
