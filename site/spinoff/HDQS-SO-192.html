The MD Image System, a true-color image processing system that serves as a diagnostic aid and tool for storage and distribution of images, was developed by Medical Image Management Systems, Huntsville, AL, as a spinoff from a spinoff." Geostar 8800 developed by Crystal Image Technologies Huntsville incorporates advanced UNIX versions of ELAS (developed by NASA's Earth Resources Laboratory for analysis of Landsat images) for general purpose image processing. The MD Image System is an application of this technology to a medical system that aids in the diagnosis of cancer and can accept store and analyze images from other sources such as Magnetic Resonance Imaging."The original spinoff
