Smartphones, laptops, and tablets are susceptible to damage if exposed to very high or low temperatures. Inspired by the "space blankets" he used as a Boy Scout, Nick Blanton, founder of Portland, Maine-based Salt Cases Company, developed fabric cases that incorporate thermal sheet technology created by Marshall Space Flight Center decades ago to protect 21st-century technology.
