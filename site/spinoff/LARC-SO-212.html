Space Age Swimsuit Reduces Drag, Breaks Records

Case number
LARC-SO-212
NASA center
LARC
Category
consumer/home/recreation
First seen
2026-07-20

Because of Langley Research Center's experience in studying the forces of friction and drag, Los Angeles-based SpeedoUSA asked the Agency to help design a swimsuit shortly after the 2004 Olympics. The LZR Racer reduces skin friction drag 24 percent more than the previous Speedo racing suit. The research seems to have paid off; in March 2008, athletes wearing the LZR Racer broke 13 world records.
