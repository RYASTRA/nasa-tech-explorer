Bioreactor Yields Extracts for Skin Cream

Case number
JSC-SO-278
NASA center
JSC
Category
consumer/home/recreation
First seen
2026-07-20

Johnson Space Flight Center researchers created a unique rotating-wall bioreactor that simulates microgravity conditions, spurring innovations in drug development and medical research. Renu_ll Int_l Inc., based in Aventure, Florida, licensed the technology and used it to produce a healing skin care product, RE`JUVEL. In a Food and Drug Administration test, RE`JUVEL substantially increased skin moisture and elasticity while reducing dark blotches and wrinkles.
