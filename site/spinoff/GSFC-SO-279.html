Esri’s Living Atlas offers a bird’s-eye view of Earth by combining free Earth-observation data from Goddard Space Flight Center with other resources. Continual updates by the Redlands, California-based company feed into apps that monitor everything from air quality and wildfires to hurricanes and shipping traffic.
