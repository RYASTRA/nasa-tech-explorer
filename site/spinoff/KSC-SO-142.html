Data Acquisition System Captures Machine Performance

Case number
KSC-SO-142
NASA center
KSC
Category
computer technology
First seen
2026-07-20

Under contract with Kennedy Space Center, Wakefield, Rhode Island-based Dewetron delivered a powerful system to monitor performance on the Space Launch System mobile launch platform. The easy-to- use system captures data with one universal signal conditioner—allowing easy analysis across multiple signals—and is now a popular part of Dewetron_s product line. Updated versions have sold to aerospace companies and car companies from Ford to BMW, as well as many other industries.
