Water-Based Coating Simplifies Circuit Board Manufacturing

Case number
GRC-SO-161
NASA center
GRC
Category
computer technology
First seen
2026-07-20

The Polymers Branch at Glenn Research Center's extensive knowledge of polyimide chemistry and its expertise in the synthesis of ultraviolet light curable polyimides was the critical component that allowed Advanced Coatings International, of Akron, Ohio, to prototype the platform chemistry for a polyimide-based, waterborne, liquid photoimagable coating ideal for the manufacture of printed circuit boards.
