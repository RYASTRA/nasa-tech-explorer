Combining ultra-high-definition video streams from multiple cameras on the NASA’s X-59 Quiet SuperSonic Technology X-plane required new video cards to process the data. A contract with Langley Research Center allowed WOLF Advanced Technology in Cleveland, Ohio to enable the near real-time video display.
