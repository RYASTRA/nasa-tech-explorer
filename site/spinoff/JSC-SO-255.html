Analyzers Provide Water Security in Space and on Earth

Case number
JSC-SO-255
NASA center
JSC
Category
environment and resource management
First seen
2026-07-20

Working with Johnson Space Center, OI Analytical, based in College Station, Texas, developed a total organic analyzer to monitor the quality of the water supply onboard the International Space Station. The company commercialized the technology, which provides significant cost savings and improved efficiency for water quality monitoring on Earth.
