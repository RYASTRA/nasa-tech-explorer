When Armstrong Flight Research Center needed a way to make temperature measurements over wide surfaces, it developed algorithms to create a flight-ready fiber-optic sensor. Sensuron of Austin, Texas, licensed the algorithms to manufacture its own fiber-optic sensing platforms.
