Diversified High Technologies, Inc. (DHT) has supported the Avionics Software Development Environment Pathfinder (ASDEP) program throughout its phases. For the first two years, DHT operated as the technology facilitator providing technical coordination between vendors, contractors, and NASA. This evolved to hands-on technical support such as systems installation, upgrades, and integration. DHT has recently been providing support in secure technology migration path planning and the incorporation of new security technologies in response to evolving program requirements.
