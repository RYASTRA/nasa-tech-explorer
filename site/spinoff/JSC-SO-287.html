Orion Parachute Innovations Carry Commercial Rockets Back to Earth

Case number
JSC-SO-287
NASA center
JSC
Category
transportation
First seen
2026-07-20

Airborne Systems Inc., whose Space and Recovery Systems branch is in Santa Ana, California, worked as a subcontractor to build the parachute system for the Orion capsule. The design is based in part on the Apollo spacecraft_s parachutes but incorporates updates and improvements requested by Johnson Space Center, which managed the contract. Johnson also carried out costly, repeated drop tests to prove the parachutes, which Airborne Systems now sells to several commercial spacecraft companies.
