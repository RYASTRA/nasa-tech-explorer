LED Lights for Sleep and Sanitizing

JSC

Healthe Inc. of Orlando, Florida, based on experience designing spacecraft lighting for NASA and using NASA-funded circadian research, developed automated LED bulbs to promote healthy sleep-wake cycles. They further developed the technology into a method of sanitizing air with ultraviolet light.
