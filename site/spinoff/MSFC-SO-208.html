Sensors Enable Plants to Text Message Farmers

Case number
MSFC-SO-208
NASA center
MSFC
Category
environment and resource management
First seen
2026-07-20

BioServe Space Technologies-a nonprofit, NASA-sponsored research partnership center-developed a leaf sensor that can monitor plants using electrical pulses, allowing anyone from astronauts to farmers to measure plant water levels directly. Berthoud, Colorado-based AgriHouse Brands Ltd. has commercialized the technology, which allows "thirsty" plants to send text messages to farmers asking for more water.
