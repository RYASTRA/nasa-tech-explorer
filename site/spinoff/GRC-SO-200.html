Concentrators Enhance Solar Power Systems

Case number
GRC-SO-200
NASA center
GRC
Category
environment and resource management
First seen
2026-07-20

Glenn Research Center and Fort Worth, Texas-based Entech Solar partnered through the SBIR program to adapt Entech's solar concentrator. Soon after developing the Solar Concentrator Array with Refractive Linear Element Technology, Entech and NASA worked on a version called the Stretched Lens Array (SLA). Recently, Entech incorporated elements from the SLA into a solar concentrator for Earth applications.
