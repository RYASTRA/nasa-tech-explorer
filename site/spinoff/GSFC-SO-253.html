Telescope Mirror Tech Improves Eye Surgery

GSFC

A system for guiding LASIK eye surgery is a by-product of early research on the James Webb Space Telescope mirrors. Santa Ana, California-based Johnson & Johnson Vision’s iDesign Refractive Studio, used by eye doctors around the world, incorporates algorithms developed by a Webb subcontractor as part of work funded by Goddard Space Flight Center.
