Cedaron Medical Inc., was founded in 1990 as a result of a NASA SBIR grant from Johnson Space Center to develop a Hand & Testing Exercise Unit for use in space. From that research came Dexter, a comprehensive workstation that creates a paperless environment for medical data management.
