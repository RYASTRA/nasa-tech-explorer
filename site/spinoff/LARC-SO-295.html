‘Digital Winglets’ for Real-Time Flight Paths

To improve aircraft efficiency and reduce emissions, Langley Research Center developed software that finds optimal routes for airplanes in flight. Through a NASA licensing agreement, APiJET LLC of Seattle built this technology into a commercial plane-routing product, allowing airlines to save both time and fuel.
