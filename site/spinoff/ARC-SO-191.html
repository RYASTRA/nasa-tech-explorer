Mars Technologies Spawn Durable Wind Turbines

Case number
ARC-SO-191
NASA center
ARC
Category
environment and resource management
First seen
2026-07-20

To develop and test wind power technology for use on Mars, Ames Research Center turned to Northern Power Systems (NPS), based in Barre, Vermont. Ames awarded NPS an SBIR contract so the company could enhance their turbine function. Today, over 200 NASA-derived Northern Power 100s are in operation on Earth and have reduced carbon emissions by 50,000 tons annually.
