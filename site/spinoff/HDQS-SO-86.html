Ship Hull Characteristics Program (SHCP) is a composite program designed to solve basic naval architecture problems and assess the structural integrity and stability of vessel design; it is used extensively by marine industry designers. SHCP is used by Brown & Root engineers as a tool in designing barges for offshore pipelaying projects.
