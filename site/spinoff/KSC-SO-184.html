A Kennedy Space Center technologist figured out how to infuse plastics with aerogel to make them better insulators. It also made them more fire-resistant, water-repellant, sound-damping, lightweight, and resistant to cryogenic temperatures. Now Okhata of Cheyenne, Wyoming, markets these “aeropolymers” to various industries.
