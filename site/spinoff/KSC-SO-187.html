Funding from the Astronaut Scholarship Foundation helped launch the Oros outdoor clothing line that incorporates aerogel insulation. Lessons about aerogel created by Kennedy Space Center led to a new non-toxic, fire-retardant formula. Now Portland, Oregon-based Solarcore has spun off from Oros to offer industry an ultra-thin insulation.
