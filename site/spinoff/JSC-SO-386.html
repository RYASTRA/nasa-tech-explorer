Boosting Bone Health

Case number
JSC-SO-386
NASA center
JSC
Category
health and medicine
First seen
2026-07-20

Because astronauts can lose bone density on long-duration missions, Johnson Space Center conducts research on bone loss. Data provided by NASA-funded research helped Osteoboost Health of Redwood City, California, develop Osteoboost, a new FDA-approved device that treats early-stage bone loss.
