Tools Ensure Reliability of Critical Software

NASA center
JPL

First seen

To demonstrate the feasibility of using a tool to automatically check software for compliance, the Jet Propulsion Laboratory awarded SBIR funding to GrammaTech Inc. of Ithaca, New York. Today, the software tool has hundreds of users worldwide, including Fortune 500 companies, educational institutions, and government agencies working on satellites, avionics, medical devices, and consumer electronics.
