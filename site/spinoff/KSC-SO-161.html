An outdoorsman couldn’t find small, well-insulated pouches to carry little items like batteries in extreme cold, so he founded Cold Case Gear in Pagosa Springs, Colorado, and released a smartphone case this year. The products rely on aerogel insulation first created under NASA SBIR contracts.
