Artificial Intelligence Targets Advertising by Understanding User

Case number
JPL-SO-198
NASA center
JPL
Category
computer technology
First seen
2026-07-20

Thousand Oaks, California startup Beyond Limits Corporation licensed an artificial intelligence program and language-understanding software from the Jet Propulsion Laboratory in 2013 and retooled the programs to target online advertising. Rather than simply remembering that a user once bought something from a particular store or pushing content based on words a user has typed, the software seeks to understand a person_s intent and sentiment at a given time and use that data to push relevant content.
