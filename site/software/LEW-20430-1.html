Lightweight Onboard Doppler Compensation for Satellite Communications

Case number
LEW-20430-1
NASA center
GRC
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

This VHDL module runs on a spacecraft's software-defined radio to reduce the effects of Doppler on wireless commnuications. The module implements a linear frequency sweep in the opposite direction of predicted Doppler shift to null out its effects on transmitted or received signals. Designed with resource-constrained transceivers in mind, minimal FPGA resources (e.g. less than 1% of Virtex 7 resources) are used.
