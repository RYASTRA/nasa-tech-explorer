The ET Toolbox system produced by the Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) for the United States Bureau of Reclamation (USBR) and Middle Rio Grande Conservancy District (MRGCD), produces daily 30 m estimates of evapotranspiration (ET) for the past seven days and future seven days in riparian areas along the Rio Grande and Colorado rivers for use in real-time water management.
