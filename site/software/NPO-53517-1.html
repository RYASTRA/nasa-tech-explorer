INvestigation of Convective UpdraftS (INCUS), Dynamic Microwave Radiometer (DMR) Level 1B Algorithm

Case number

NASA center

First seen

The algorithm processes the spacecraft telemetry to provide an estimate of geolocation and other relevant observing geometry needed for understanding of the data. The main purpose of the algorithm is to transform the radiometric counts to the antenna temperature and brightness temperature. The algorithm also flags the data for quality control and creates netCDF output files.
