iMMOD (Interactive Model of Mosquito Distribution)

Case number

NASA center
LARC
Category
data and image processing

This software visualizes NASA Earth observations, citizen science and public health data relevant to mosquito habitat suitability by season. This software also implements a seasonal model to predict habitat suitability for mosquitoes in Western Europe.
