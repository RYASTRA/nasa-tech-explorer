GPS Occultation Analysis System (GOAS)

Case number
NPO-30596-1
NASA center
JPL
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

GOAS processes atmospheric and ionospheric occultation data obtained from low-Earth-orbiting global positioning systems transmission receivers. The technology obtains input from a variety of receiver types and satellites and outputs full atmospheric and ionospheric retrievals.
