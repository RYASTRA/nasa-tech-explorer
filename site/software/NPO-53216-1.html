Plant Trait Estimation from Radiance for Fire Risk Estimation

Case number
NPO-53216-1
NASA center
JPL
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

Existing plant traits estimation techniques rely on empirical models so they are sensitive to the training data. Here we use a physical model that estimate reflectance, and we use that reflectance within a radiance model to invert radiance measurements directly to plant traits.
