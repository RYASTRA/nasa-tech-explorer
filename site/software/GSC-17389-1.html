The Global Learning and Observations to Benefit the Environment (GLOBE) program is a worldwide hands-on, primary and secondary school-based science and education program. The GLOBE Program's Data Entry App for iOS, enables members to enter data to via their iOS devices and when in the field, where they may not have internet connection. Their data will be stored locally on their iOS devices and sent to the GLOBE database once their devices get connected to internet. This project also facilitates data entry by leveraging mobile capabilities including GPS, photography and date/time.
