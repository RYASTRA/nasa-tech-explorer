MATLAB-Oslo Toolkit

Case number
GSC-15138-1
NASA center
GSFC
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

This toolkit is a set of MATLAB scripts and functions that enable rapid transfer of optical system and performance data from Oslo optical software into the MATLAB environment. Typical applications include: extracting prescription data into MATLAB to confirm consistency of various delivered models; perturbing the models and performing various analyses in support of integrated modeling activities; and enabling a MATLAB-driven optical model for integrated system-level modeling of wavefront sensing and control. The toolkit can be downloaded at http://opensource.gsfc.nasa.gov/projects/Matlab/index.php
