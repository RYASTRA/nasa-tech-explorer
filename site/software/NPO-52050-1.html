SAR Exploration Notebooks

Case number
NPO-52050-1
NASA center
JPL
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

The notebooks enable quick visualization and interpretation of false-color SAR RGB images, and InSAR correlation images. Notebooks are run in Google Colab and the first cells will set up the python environment. Users are asked to upload image samples into their Google Drive for easy access from Google Colab.
