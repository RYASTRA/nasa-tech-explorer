Meteoroid Engineering Model (MEM) version 3

MEM 3 is NASA's most current and accurate model of the meteoroid environment. It describes potentially hazardous meteoroids in the inner solar system and provides the flux, directionality, speed, and bulk density of meteoroids encountered along a user-specified spacecraft trajectory.
