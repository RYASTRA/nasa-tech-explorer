Teamtools Studio Suite and DemoSat Adaptation

This software is meant to enable spacecraft operators and operators of other complex systems to build last-mile solutions around existing Ground Data System solutions (GDS).It provides a wide range of functionalities across the Planning, Scheduling, Sequencing, Sequence Simulation, Sequence Validation, and Downlink Analysis functions of spacecraft operations. By bridging the gap between rigid, high-complexity Ground Data Systems and the dynamic workflow of the human operator, the software ensures that "quality of life" tools do not introduce operational risk. It replaces ad-hoc, untraceable manual scripts with a unified, tested framework, automating routine decision-making while maintaining a enabling users to create a complete audit trail of every operational action taken.
