Magnetosphere Multi-Scale (MMS) Spacecraft Flight Software

Case number
GSC-16471-1
NASA center
GSFC
Category
vehicle management space air ground
First seen
2026-07-20

This spacecraft flight software was developed to support unique multi-satellite MMS mission requirements. The technology interfaces to a sensor/actuator complement that includes a star sensor and Digital Sun Sensor, Accelerometer, and Thruster hardware to implement the functionality for determining and controlling spacecraft attitude and orbit.
