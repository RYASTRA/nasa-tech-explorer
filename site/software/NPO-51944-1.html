For engineers and non-engineers who work with CAD models, ProtoSpace is a multi-platform tool that allows users to collectively view and manipulate models to save cost and time in the design and assembly processes. Unlike existing CAD tools that cannot support collaborative, persistent sessions in augmented reality (AR) and on the web, ProtoSpace collectively sees and solves problems before they happen. The ProtoSpace system not only accommodates engineers working with CAD, but also consists of a procedural training system, PICARD, that allows for users to train and execute procedures using the ProtoSpace system, both in augmented reality (AR) and on a web-training platform.
