AMMOS Key Management and Cryptography (KMC)

Category

KMC is used in flight project ground data systems to utilize cryptography to protect the confidentiality and integrity of data at-rest and in-transit.KMC also provides features that are used in flight project ground data systems to apply frame-layer encryption and/or authentication to protect frames being uplinked to flight systems.
