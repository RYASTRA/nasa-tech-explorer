Chesapeake Bay Chlorophyll Hotspot Identifier (CBCHI)

Case number
LAR-18794-1
NASA center
LARC
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

The Chesapeake Bay Chlorophyll Hotspot Identifier takes in raw Landsat 8 surface reflectance product and produces two maps to be opened in ArcMap that can be used to identify chlorophyll hotspots. It also creates a true color image.
