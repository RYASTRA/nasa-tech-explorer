Co-SWOT (Coastal Surface Water and Ocean Topography)

The Surface Water and Ocean Topography missions surveys global water bodies including oceans, lakes, rivers, reservoirs, and wetlands. Coastal areas, such as estuaries and deltas, present a specific challenge due to the interaction of marine and terrestrial forces and the complex geomorphology. Both LR and HR data products are available over coastal areas, but both require several steps to process and evaluate accuracy of SWOT measurements of water surface elevations. To streamline processing of data within coastal areas (or anywhere that SWOT data are collected), we've built a set of tools to download, filter, and evaluate SWOT products.
