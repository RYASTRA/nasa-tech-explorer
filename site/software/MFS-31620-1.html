Cosmic Ray Effects On Microelectronics (CREME96)

Case number
MFS-31620-1
NASA center
MSFC
Category
system testing
First seen
2026-07-20

This software is an improved model of the ionizing radiaiton environment in space and its effects on satellites and is a computational tool used by aerospace engineers. This software is used to estimate single event effects (SEE) rates: 1) as a guide to parts selection for spacecraft, 2) to demonstrate compliance of a proposed spacecraft design with engineering requirements on reliability and operability, and 3) as a guide in determining the cause of observed spacecraft anomalies.
