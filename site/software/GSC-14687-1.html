Goddard Enhanced Onboard Navigation System (GEONS)

Case number
GSC-14687-1
NASA center
GSFC
Category
operations
First seen
2026-07-20

GEONS processes data from standard GPS receivers, communication equipment, and/or attitude sensors to produce accurate, absolute, relative onboard navigation solutions in real time. Navigation products from GEONS support additional autonomous functions, including onboard maneuver control, science viewing, and relative navigation for formation keeping.
