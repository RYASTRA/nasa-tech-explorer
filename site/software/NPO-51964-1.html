The Open Archival Information System Interoperability Framework (OAIS-IF) prototype serves as a test implementation of the OAIS-IF framework. Its goal is to facilitate international and collaborative research by fostering data sharing. Its primary objective is to offer a collection of interoperable protocols and interface specifications, enabling seamless access and reuse of data both within and beyond the digital repositories' operational boundaries. The OAIS-IF is intentionally designed to be versatile, catering to a wide range of small, medium, and large-scale use cases, disciplines, and stakeholders. Furthermore, it complements the overarching OAIS Reference Model (ISO 14721).
