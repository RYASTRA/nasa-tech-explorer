Delay Tolerant Network Marshall Enterprise (DTNME) is a NASA supported software for payload operations to support reliable space communications. It is fully CCSDS compliant. This software is continuously updated with new technologies to further support and enhance its operational usage.The Bundle Restaging Daemon (BRD) is a new DTNME technology feature that allows operations to have better control over different data streams within a DTN system. Additionally, BRD provides safeguards to lessen the potential impact of any particular data stream against another.
