GSFC

The Pan Galactic Engineering Framework provides a desktop client with a network interface and a network-accessible repository service that together implement a collaborative Model-Based Engineering (MBE) capability. Its initial implementation goal is to support a concurrent conceptual design process for engineering and science teams developing early phase space mission designs.
