Derived meteorological products for stratospheric observations

Case number
NPO-53549-1
NASA center
JPL
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

This software interpolate meteorological fields (such as, temperature, wind speed, and ozone) to match the times and locations of atmospheric measurements. These routines also derive additional variables, including but not limited to static stability, potential temperature, equivalent latitude, and characterization of the jets and tropopauses.
