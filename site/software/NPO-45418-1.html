Planetary Observer Planning Software (POPS)

Case number
NPO-45418-1
NASA center
JPL
Category
autonomous systems
First seen
2026-07-20

The POPS application suite performs mission design analysis of a spacecraft orbiting a terrestrial planet. POPS is not intended for the study of hyperbolic or interplanetary orbits, although some limited hyperbolic capabilities exist.
