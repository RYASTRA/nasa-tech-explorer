Spot the Station (STS) is a web-based application that notifies people via text or email when ISS is visible overhead from a designated location. STMD partnered with SOMD to develop a more modern mobile app version of STS. Development is under way with publication scheduled for May 2023. The mobile app functions similarly to the web application in terms of providing user notifications of location-specific siting opportunities, but also has A/R capabilities to enhance the user experience.
