The Orbit Determination Toolbox (ODTBX) R2020a (v8.1) is an advanced mission simulation and analysis tool used for concept exploration, proposal, early design phase, or rapid design center environments; the emphasis is on flexibility but it has enough fidelity to produce credible results. ODTBX v8.1 includes multiple feature additions, enhancements, and bug fixes from the prior release. The primary user interface and supporting functions are written in Matlab and Java.
