The Multi-mission Algorithm and Analytics Platform (MAAP)

Case number
NPO-50982-1
NASA center
JPL
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

The purpose of the joint NASA/ESA Multi-Mission Algorithm and Analysis Platform (MAAP) is to maximize the use of earth observational data in an open and collaborative environment. Initially, the project will use data from the BIOMASS, GEDI, and NISAR missions, which represent a highly complementary set of measurements to determine Earths aboveground woody biomass with the goal of expanding as a general purpose platform for analyzing earth science data. The capabilities envisioned for the MAAP will enable for the first time efficient and tailorable comparisons, cross calibration, and fusion of data from these missions for a broad community of scientists and other users.
