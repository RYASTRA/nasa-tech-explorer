Integrated Test and Operations System (ITOS) is a comprehensive telemetry and command (T&C) solution for spacecraft, component, and instrument development, integration, test, and mission operations. It is highly user-configurable, and provides a scalable, cost-effective platform for small-budget projects to billion-dollar observatories. ITOS also provides extensive, closed-loop, scripted simulation capabilities.
