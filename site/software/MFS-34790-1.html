Dyreqt is developed to provide a general capability for synthesis of space transportation architectures in the pre-conceptual and conceptual phases of design. Rather than focusing on sizing, the primary aim of Dyreqt is enabling flexible synthesis of any arbitrary architecture element models that may be described within Dyreqts ontology. In doing this, Dyreqt enables the rapid exploration and optimization of architecture concepts subject to parametric requirements regardless of the high-/low-/mixed-/multi-fidelity nature of the models used.
