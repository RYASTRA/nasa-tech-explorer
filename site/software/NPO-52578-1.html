The Air Quality Analytic Collaborative Framework (AQACF) Health Impact Calculator is a set of Python scripts and Jupyter notebook for calculating air quality health impacts for major cities from GEOS-Chem adjoint sensitivities. It currently supports 14 cities in the US but could be extended.
