The NISAR Quality Assurance (QA) software provides detailed validation and verification of the science data products for the NASA-ISRO Synthetic Aperture Radar (NISAR) mission. It is designed for use by scientists, the NISAR algorithm development team, the NISAR mission Science Data System, and other users in the NISAR community. With the QA software, these communities can take input NISAR science data products and quickly generate plots, reports, quality metrics, quick-look images, verification check outcomes, and more on those input science products. The QA outputs are in human- and machine-readable formats, such as PNG, PDF, CSV, and HDF5.
