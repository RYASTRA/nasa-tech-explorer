Landslide Hazard Assessment for Situational Awareness (LHASA)

Case number
GSC-17452-1
NASA center
GSFC

First seen
2026-07-20

LHASA was created at Goddard Space Flight Center to identify the potential for rainfall-triggered landslides in real time. LHASA version 2 adopts machine learning to estimate the probability of landslide occurrence at a 30-arcsecond (~1 km) daily resolution. In addition, it estimates the potential exposure of human population and roads to landslide hazard.
