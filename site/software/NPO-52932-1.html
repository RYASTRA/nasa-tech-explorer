This software cleans and calibrates images produced by the Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope Coronagraph Instrument's Exoplanetary Systems Camera (EXCAM). Roman is an upcoming NASA astrophysics observatory, and EXCAM is the "science" camera for the Coronagraph Instrument onboard Roman. EXCAM uses a visible-light CCD sensor (not ITAR-controlled).
