CODA: Collaborative Operations Data Activation

Case number
MSC-27338-1
NASA center
JSC
Category
design and integration tools
First seen
2026-07-20

CODA allows replay of every moment of missions, training, and testing by pulling data from existing sources such as video, audio and imagery servers, GPS position, and timestamped text databases and displays them in context with each other. Consolidate the context of missions, training, and testing into an easy to use platform to relive and revisit each moment. CODA aims to aggregate data records ranging from video, audio, telemetry, still imagery, anomaly reporting, and more. Heavily inspired by Apollo in Real Time (https://apolloinrealtime.org/), which was created by the primary CODA developer, Ben Feist.
