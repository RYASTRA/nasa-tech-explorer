The Science Collaboration and data Interaction eXtended Reality tool or SciXR is the combination of the SciVR (NTR 50913) virtual reality tool and SciAR (NTR 51857) augmented reality tool for immersive science data exploration and analysis. SciXR provides a rich, real-time science data visualization engine in an interactive extended reality (XR) environment for use by scientists.
