This software will be used by the SWOT project, the SWOT Algorithm Development Team, the SWOT Calibration and Vaidation Team, The SWOT Science Team and the Hydrology Community in general to analyze, interpret and filter the SWOT river data to study both the limits of the SWOT measurement, and the river hydrology science that can be done with SWOT data.
