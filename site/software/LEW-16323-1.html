Axial-Flow Turbine Off-Design (AXOD) Performance

Case number
LEW-16323-1
NASA center
GRC
Category
propulsion
First seen
2026-07-20

AXOD computes the flow and efficiency of multistage axial-flow turbines as functions of speed and pressure ratio. The technology uses a span-line analysis with simple radial equilibrium. The loss model includes blade-row inlet losses, blade-row losses, and stage-test losses. Coefficients are selected to match the known design-point performance, and the internal model provides the off-design performance.
