Inverse Heat Conduction Solver Using MATLAB - IHC Solve

Case number
LAR-18924-1
NASA center
LARC
Category
aeronautics
First seen
2026-07-20

This MATLAB software first reads in temperature data measured from two thermocouples embedded in a heat conducting specimen. The software also reads in the thermophysical properties for the heat conducting specimen. After filtering noise from the measured temperature data, the software then uses the filtered data and thermophysical properties to solve both a direct and inverse heat conduction problem to estimate both temperature and heat flux on the external surface of the specimen.
