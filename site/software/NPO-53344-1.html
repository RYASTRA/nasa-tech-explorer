Weak Lensing Cosmological Inference Software Pipeline for Roman Space Telescope's High Latitude Imaging Survey

Case number
NPO-53344-1
NASA center
JPL
Category

First seen
2026-07-20

This cosmology pipeline is specifically tailored to extract cosmological information (in the form of estimates of cosmological parameters) from the Roman Space Telescope High Latitude Imaging Survey data. This software pipeline will enable the Roman high-latitude imaging survey to achieve its extraordinary cosmological potential, while simultaneously providing unprecedented volumes of exquisite data for an enormous range of astrophysical investigations.
