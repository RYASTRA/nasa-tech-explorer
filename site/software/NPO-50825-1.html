Asteroid Survey Simulation Tool

Case number
NPO-50825-1
NASA center
JPL
Category
data and image processing
First seen
2026-07-20

Modeling of candidate asteroid search programs by producing files of individual image pointing for the observatory. Hypothetical asteroid populations are checked to see which of the objects are in each field and lists of candidate detections are developed. Post-processing leads to an estimate of the programs search performance.
