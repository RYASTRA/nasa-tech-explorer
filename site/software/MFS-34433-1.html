Large Scale Data Transfer Study

NASA center

Category

First seen

To facilitate petabyte scale data transfer between NASA and ESA, we have developed a framework for transferring 6 petabytes of data from ESA to NASA to support the Harmonized Landsat Sentinel-2 data products. With increases in collaboration between the agencies and increased data volumes, this framework is designed such that it can be applied for future data exchanges between the agencies.
