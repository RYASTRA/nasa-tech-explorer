The Ocean Ecology Lab (NASA/GSFC/OEL) develops computational notebooks that demonstrate the access and use of data products from the Ocean Biology Distributed Active Archive Center (OB.DAAC). Notebooks are files containing code and computational results (including data visualizations) saved in the Jupyter format and distributed as downloads from and also rendered on the OB.DAAC website at https://oceandata.sci.gsfc.nasa.gov. The oceandata notebook collection demonstrates efficient data access patterns and processing codes, current with the latest provisioning and processing services offered by the OB.DAAC and relevant Earth Science Data Systems (ESDS) projects, for the benefit of a broad community of ocean and atmospheric research and monitoring programs. In turn, and exemplifying NASA's commitments to open source science, this external community is invited to contribute improvements to the oceandata notebook collection through the source code repository. The notebook collection is a product of the Plankton, Aerosol, Cloud, and ocean Ecosystem (PACE) mission and has no intentional commercial applications.
