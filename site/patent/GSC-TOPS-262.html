Alternative Transparent Coating Lotus Suitable for Optics with Vacuum Deposition Layer

Case number
GSC-TOPS-262
NASA center
GSFC
Category
materials and coatings
First seen
2026-07-20

NASA instrumentation is at risk for contamination from dusty space environments. Additionally, contamination from water and ice buildup can affect instrumentation function. The innovation of the LOTUS coating prevents contamination on sensitive surfaces, like optics, that cannot be cleaned during space missions.
