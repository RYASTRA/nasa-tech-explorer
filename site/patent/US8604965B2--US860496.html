Virtual Aircraft Target Generator for ADS-B Testing

Case number
US8604965B2
NASA center
FAA
Category
aerospace
First seen
2026-07-20

This patented system, developed by the Federal Aviation Administration, enables the generation of virtual aircraft targets for safe and efficient testing of avionics systems, such as satellite-based surveillance, including ADS-B. By simulating real aircraft using RF signals embedded with synthetic GPS data, the system allows flight crews and developers to validate surveillance system performance in realistic but controlled environments without deploying additional aircraft.
