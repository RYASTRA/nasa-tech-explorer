STELLA-1.2 is an innovative low-cost, modular, open-source sensor suite designed for environmental and spectral analysis. The platform supports interchangeable sensor cartridges that attach magnetically to a central data-logging base unit, enabling users to collect air quality, spectral, and geospatial data in a flexible and accessible manner. Designed with affordability and accessibility in mind, STELLA-1.2 is built from commercially available components and can be assembled using 3D-printed snap-together parts without fasteners.
